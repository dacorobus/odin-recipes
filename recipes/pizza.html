My Pizza Recipe

Description

Home made pizza is the best. I use nan bread, a classic pizza sauce, marble cheese with different fixing to produce a mouth watering pizza in just a few minutes. Once the pizza and ingredients are ready, I place it in the oven, which was preheated to 425 degrees, for 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • nan bread
  • Presidents Choice Pizza Sauce
  • Toppings of your choice
  • Shredded Marble Cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat over to 425 degrees celsius
  2. Use 1 or 2 six inch nan bread
  3. Cover nan bread with sauce
  4. Add toppings
  5. Add cheese
  6. Place in over for 10 minutes
